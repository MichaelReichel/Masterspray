Common Questions

Do you offer free repair quotes?

Yes! You can visit our workshop for a free assessment, or send us photos via our "Get a Quote" system for a preliminary estimate.

Do you work with my insurance company?

We are an approved repairer for all major insurance companies in Namibia. We handle the claims process for you to make it stress-free.

How long does a respray usually take?

A high-quality respray typically takes 3 to 7 working days, depending on the car's size and the level of prep work required.

Do you provide a warranty on paintwork?

Absolutely. We offer a lifetime warranty on paint work, ensuring your car stays beautiful for years to come.
